## About

I'm a **Senior Site Reliability Engineer at Red Hat**, currently focused on platform reliability and scalability. Previously, I was a Principal Engineer on the **OpenShift Networking** team, working deep in OVN-Kubernetes, service mesh, ingress routing, and multi-cloud infrastructure deployments.

I also run **[Art of Exploitation](https://www.youtube.com/@artofexploitation-ay)** — a YouTube channel covering networking, OpenShift internals, and hands-on technical deep dives.

I write detailed, hands-on guides because I believe good documentation cuts through hours of trial and error. Most of my public work lives here and at my [work GitHub](https://github.com/aygarg-rh).
